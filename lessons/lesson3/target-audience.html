Target Audience Description

The target audience of "Lodi can do it":

  • Who: The members of Lodi can do it are a very multicultural and diverse public such as business owners, education associations, shopping centers, parks, lawyers, libraries, local universities designing partners, media partners, home realty groups, international and local banks, dental associations Sewer and water companies, restaurants, kids entertainment and more.
  • Age: 8 and up.
  • Technology: The mayority of the audience will access the site through mobile devices, tablets and laptops. Senior citizens will probably use desktops of their own preference.
  • Motivation: Lodi can do it develops working relationships with its local government, board of education and other organizations in Lodi to create a community that thrives because of its engaging residents. Some of the activities that they do are community events where people of all ages are invited to enjoy and they also advocate, promote and help form new local volunteer Non-profit organizations raising awareness of Mental Health, Diversity, Equality and Inclusion through the Borough of Lodi and its Stigma Free Initiative.
