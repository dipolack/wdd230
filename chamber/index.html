About us

We pride ourselves with assisting the businesses in our greater area through trainings, seminars and networking events as well as creating a positive impact through community relations through events and contests to establish a long term relationship with the residents of our region. Working together with the municipality, we pledge to provide a clean, safe environment for our customers. We will work with the local officials to ensure responsible government and improvements to the borough of Lodi and its neighbors.
