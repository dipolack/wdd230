Whitespace

Lush

lushusa.com/

This website is a nice example of whitespace because it has a vibrant pop of color highlighting the vibrant products they offer in a white background while being contrasted with large black and white typography and a black header which pop to the eye while keeping it easily readable.

Proximity

Jesper Gadeberg Music Stylist

jespergadeberg.com/

This website is a good example of how proximity can be used to succesfully achieve an effective visual design while showcasing similar design elements close together to the user.

Contrast

Illo

illo.tv/studio

This is a great example of contrast being used throughout a website. This website has many contrasting elements and media-types such as Illustrations, wide photos, rows of text and square portraits with colored backgrounds. The smart use of colors while keeping a white background and unique patents allows this website to be easily readable.
